GRAMMAR

Noun

It is a word (other than a pronoun) used to identify any of a class of people, places, or things common noun, or to name a particular one of these proper noun.

Verb

It is a word used to describe an action, state, or occurrence, and forming the main part of the predicate of a sentence, such as hear, become, happen.

Adjective

It is a word or phrase naming an attribute, added to or grammatically related to a noun to modify or describe it.
